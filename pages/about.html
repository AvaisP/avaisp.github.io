About

I am a third year Computer Engineering student currently studying at D.J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, Mumbai. I am interested in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. Additionally, I am a fairly decent Python and Javascript developer. I've been selected as one of the students for Google Summer of Code 2017. You can check out my GSoC posts here. Outside of the digital world, I am a foodie. I love going out to eat (chicken?) at nice places (especially if they are easy on the pocket :P).

This blog is a means for me to share what I know. I will be writing about my GSoC project weekly. In addition to that, I will also be writing tutorials. If you are a Computer Engineering student from a Tier 2 or 3 college who is as lost as I was when I started off, make sure you follow this blog!
